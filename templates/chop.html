Chop Chop

Chop Chop was my team's submission to Innovation in Action, a six-month entrepreneurial experience to solve real-world public health problems. We were awarded 3rd Place and People's Choice awards totalling $6,000 to pursue the idea. An LLC was formed and members of the team continue to work on its renamed iteration, Skillet.

Chop Chop is a dinnerkit available through convenient kiosk locations. Offering grab-and-go box of fresh ingredients with a recipe that helps grow your nutrition and cooking literacy, Chop Chop helps bring back dinner to the many of us who are often stuck with less healthy options.

Skills or Knowledge Gained:

  • Customer discovery
  • Business model development
  • Cross-discipline teamwork
  • Startup pitching process
  • Nutrition literacy
  • Branding and design
