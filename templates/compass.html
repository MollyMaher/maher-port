Compass

In an entreprenuership in information course, my partner and I worked on tackling the issue of patient educaiton in complex and pediatric hospital environments. Piggybacking off my work in bone marrow transplant, we conducted KJ Method research on issue faced by clinical staff in this hospital unit.

We developed a prototype of Compass, a clinician-facing task management tool that helps medical professionals educate patients. Unlike traditional electronic health record software, BMT Compass focuses on what our target users say they want- discrete steps instead of long form documentation. By offering an easy-to-use system that reports back to proprietary EHRs, we empower staff to better educate patients and ultimately improve outcomes for families.
